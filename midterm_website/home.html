Scuderia Ferrari, An Introduction

Ferrari is by far the most iconic and important name in motorsport and luxury and automotive history. Built on Passion, Performance, and Perfection, Ferrari's distinctive style has been present since its founding as a racing team by visionary Enzo Ferrari in 1929. Deeply rooted in racing and history, the Maranello-based team has built a legacy of producing the best luxury road cars and the fastest racecars alike. with almost a century of excellence, Ferrari is immortalized as a true cornerstone of automotive history.
